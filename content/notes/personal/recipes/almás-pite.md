# almás pite

## general notes

fills a 13x9 inch pan

45 min prep, 40-50 min bake @ 355 F / 180 C

for this amount, you need 2 eggs: 1.5 goes into the dough EXACTLY and the other 0.5 can go for glaze/brush at the end.

the flour is underestimated, since you can always add more. add enough to make it "doughy" and easy to work with

however, when working the dough, DONT kneed it excessively. just work it and mix the materials, which results in a more pastry-like crust rather than a thick doughy-crust 

## ingredients 

### dough

* 375-400 g fine flour
* 103 g sugar
* 103 g butter
* 4 g baking powder
* 1.5 eggs (NO MORE/LESS)
* 120 g sour cream
* 0.7 pinch salt
* 0.5 egg (for brushing)

### filling

* 2 kg apples
* 70–80 g sugar
* 85 g walnuts
* 2.5 tsp cinnamon
* 1 lemon zest

## instructions

1. refine flour, and add all dough ingredients. make sure butter is soft
2. mix well, and dont kneed for too long/hard. add more flour accordingly
3. cover and place in fridge for minimum 30-40 minutes
4. while the dough rests, peel all apples and grate them, medium coarseness
5. squeeze all the liquid out with hands, filter. (apple juice and you can drink it/keep for later!)
6. add and mix sugar / cinnamon / lemon zest into shredded apple
7. finely shred the walnuts. dont chop, too coarse!
8. butter the pan (or cooking spray). get dough, spread thin into pan
9. put thin layer of walnuts, followed by shredded apple filling
10. thin layer of dough to cover the top, use a fork to puncture holes in it, brush the  scrambled egg to glaze
11. bake at 355 F / 180 C for 40-50 minutes, until the glaze gets golden brown
12. enjoy
